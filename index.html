05 · How I Think

Product
Philosophy

01

Metrics are the product.

Every feature decision starts with a clear success metric — not a KPI slide, a real number that changes a business. If I can't define what winning looks like in measurable terms before building, I don't build.

02

Friction is the enemy.

The best product experience is invisible. Whether it's a booking flow, a search result, or a payment screen — I obsess over every millisecond and click between the user and their goal. Speed is a feature.

03

AI is a multiplier, not a strategy.

GenAI products I've shipped weren't built because AI was trending. They were built because a real user problem existed, and AI was the best solution. I build AI where it creates measurable outcomes.
